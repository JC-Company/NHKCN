About Us

Welcomes Christians and those who seek to connect to Christianity in the Lakewood area.

We aim to make contact with and encourage others to join us in our lifechanging Christian journey.

We are a friendly Christian community where we welcome others to join us in our worship and service to God.

Our vision it to make an impact for God, here in Lakewood, Washington by helping people understand the enriching messages of eternal hope given to us by Jesus Christ through His words and deeds.

Everyone welcome, no matter your age, beliefs, or background. Come just as you are -  we'd love to get to know you better.
